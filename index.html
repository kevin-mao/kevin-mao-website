Paean Health

Paean Health (Nicolas Acosta ’21, Vincent Guo ’21, Kevin Mao ’21, and Alaz Sengul ’21) is a new start-up that won funding through the Columbia Engineering Design Challenge. We are tackling the opioid criss by developing the X-Pouch, a small insert for prescription pill bottles that will deactivate unused pills for safe disposal.

NMR spectra of isotropic bicelles

At the Tang Lab, at the College of Staten Island, I contributed to research on the effect of ions on the NMR spectra of bicelles. Bicelles are rings of fat molecules that mimic the cellular membrane and serve as useful platforms to study membrane proteins. The research was published here.
